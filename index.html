Hoi An considered one of Vietnam’s most picturesque towns. Joining half day Hoi An city tour, you'll meet with a knowledgeable local guide for introductory and learn all about its illustrious past as a trading port...
Beside that you have chance to taste & explore culinary delights the local food . Let’s join us and discover the charming little city and specialities cuisine through Hoi An city tour.

Details:

Pickup

Pick up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An ) and our lovely guide will takes you to Hoi an ancient town. It is one of the major trading centers in Southeast Asia during the 16th century due to the early western trader occupying period.

Action

With the guide, you will walk to visit The Japanese Covered Bridge; The Assembly Hall; The Old House of Tan Ky ; Museums and Hoi An Traditional Art Performance House and many Buddhist pagodas on the side, Hoi an market,..and learn about the daily life of Hoi an local people.

Action

Enjoy the most authentic Hoi An specialties from our favorite vendors, who have been feeding locals for generations. You will taste 4 specialities in Hoi An when walking in the ancient town : Hoi An Rice noodles (Cao Lầu), Bread (Bánh Mì), Rice Pancake (Bánh Xèo) and Grilled Pork (Thịt nướng ).
